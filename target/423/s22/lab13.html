CSE 423 Lab #13: Fun with Reverse Engineering

Project Name: Investigating the Use of Web-Based Coding Tutorials in OOP Education

Students will be using a web-based Java tutorial system developed by a team at NMT. The study should take no longer than 50 minutes to complete and will consist of asking each student to sign a consent form (they are not required to sign the form if they do not wish to participate in the study), fill out a brief entry survey, use the system, and fill out an exit survey.
